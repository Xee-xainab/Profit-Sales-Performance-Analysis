                    Profit & Sales Performance Dashboard
Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance, profitability, order status, and branch performance using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides an interactive view of key business metrics and helps identify revenue drivers, operational challenges, and growth opportunities.

Objectives
Monitor overall sales and profit performance.
Evaluate branch-level revenue contribution.
Analyze product category performance.
Track order fulfillment status.
Identify revenue trends over time.

Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Pivot Charts
Slicers
Dashboard Design
Dashboard Overview

The dashboard tracks:

Total Revenue: ₦251,592
Total Profit: ₦95,462
Total Orders: 500
Total Quantity Sold: 1,560

Key Insights
Lagos emerged as the highest revenue-generating branch, contributing ₦57,143 in sales.
Electronics was the top-performing category, generating ₦74,107 in revenue and ₦28,104 in profit.
Fashion closely followed Electronics as a major contributor to overall sales.
Pending orders (136) exceeded delivered orders (115), highlighting potential inefficiencies in the fulfillment process.
Clothing generated the lowest revenue and profit among all product categories.
Monthly revenue fluctuated throughout the year, indicating possible seasonal demand patterns.

Recommendations
Improve order processing and delivery operations to reduce pending and cancelled orders.
Leverage successful sales strategies from Lagos in lower-performing branches.
Increase marketing efforts for high-performing categories such as Electronics and Fashion.
Investigate the causes of low Clothing sales and implement targeted improvement strategies.
Develop promotional campaigns during low-sales periods to stabilize revenue.

Conclusion

The analysis reveals a profitable business with strong performance in Electronics and Fashion categories. While Lagos leads revenue generation, operational improvements are needed to address the high number of pending and cancelled orders. By optimizing fulfillment processes and focusing on top-performing products and branches, the business can further improve profitability and customer satisfaction.
